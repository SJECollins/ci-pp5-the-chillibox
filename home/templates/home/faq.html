Products

All the seeds we sell in our shop are from plants that we cultivate in our greenhouses here in Ireland!

Each seed pack contains your seeds, carefully packaged to keep them safe and dry. We recommend when potting your seeds to use about three seeds per pot so we package accordingly. Small packs contain roughly a dozen seeds. Medium or Standard packs contain around 24. Large packs contain about 36. Of course, seeds are small so these numbers aren't exact.

Seedboxes contain seed packs for each of the chilli pepper varieties you've selected, you just need to select which size seed packs you'd like. Also included is our handy booklet which contains lots of helpful tips for growing your chilli peppers!

Orders

We aim to dispatch your order ASAP. Orders placed before 2pm are dispatched the same day. Within Ireland, delivery takes about 5-7 business days. Internationally, it make take a little longer. Please contact us if you have any concerns about your order.

If you have placed your order but want to make a change, please contact us and we will do whatever we can to help.

To cancel your order, please contact us and we will do whatever we can to help.
